SCHOOL LIFE

school library

Opened In 2010

It is a Netflix of books, filled with every type of books, newspapers or even a ipad section where children can research for their school projects for a limited amount of time. If children have a EZ link card, they can borrow up to 5 books at a time for a week for free! So send your child to school with a EZ link card and wait for them to come back to see What wonderful books we have in store!

school canteen

Opened In 2007

We have 7 different stalls in our canten, which all serve a delicious amount of food. We sell food at a 40% price of what you pay to get the same food outside. Your child can also play in the canteen but no running is allowed. There will be some prefects at the canteen who will make sure that everything is right. If it is your child's very first day of school at grade 1 then you can stand at the canteen and instruct him on how to buy the food.Have fun at our canteen, kids!!!

school computer lab

Opened In 2014

We have 3 computer labs, which have 40 computers each, making sure that everyone gets a computer each. We will do a lot of stuff there, such as logging into your student account and your google classroom. We also do some online assignments for the students there. Each laptop even has a set of headphones!! If your child has a problem about the computer or IT stuff, there will be a group of people who make sure that everything is up and running. have fun at the computer lab!!!
